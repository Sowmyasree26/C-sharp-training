Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He is known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology.

Achievements:

  • Played a key role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.
  • Authored several inspirational books like "Wings of Fire".
  • Served as President of India from 2002 to 2007.
